How to create a vegetable garden

Planning the Garden

If you want to have a vegetable garden, you can not forget about planning! Here are some basic steps to keep in mind.

  1. Decide what to grow
  2. Select a garden area
  3. Design a garden

Getting ready to plant

Before you have a nice garden prepare yourself, with following steps

  1. Buy seeds and supplies
  2. Tiller the soil
  3. Get the soil ready

Growing the vegetables

Growing vegetables is not hard if you remember about following steps:

  1. Dig holes and plant the seeds or seedlings
  2. Water the garden
  3. Weed the garden
  4. Keep critters away
  5. Care for the vegetables according to their needs
